These are the three types of volcanoes

Ash volcanoes

volcanoes formed from ash are usually steep-sided. They often have a large crator. An example of an ash volcano is the paricutin ash volcano in Mexico.


Lava volcano 1

There are two types of lava volcano, lava volcano 1 and lava volcano 2 here we will be talking about lava volcano 1. when thick and slow moving lava flows it hardens quickly to form a steep-sided volcano this is known as lava volcano 1. an example of lava volcano 1 is the Cotopaxi volcano in Ecuador.
